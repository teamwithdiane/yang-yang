Life at Garden Oasis

Amenities & services, in full

From the first meal of the day to the last light in the garden, every part of life here is considered. Here's everything included in a Garden Oasis residency.

Dining

01

Chef-prepared meals

Fresh, seasonal dishes served three times daily in our private dining room.

Dietary accommodations

Individual meal plans handled quietly, from allergies to personal preferences.

Snacks & hydration

Available around the clock in every common room and kitchenette.

Care & wellness

02

24-hour care staff

A steady, familiar team on-site day and night for support and peace of mind.

On-site nursing

Medication management and health monitoring handled by our nursing team.

Emergency call system

In every suite, so help is always a button press away.

Escort to meals & activities

A hand to hold and a familiar face for the walk to any part of the community.

Secure environment

Thoughtfully designed and monitored for safety without feeling institutional.

Comfort & daily living

03

Private & companion suites

Choose the arrangement that feels most like home.

Housekeeping, laundry & linens

Handled weekly so residents can spend their time on what matters to them.

Complimentary Wi-Fi & TV

Stay connected with family and favorite shows from every suite.

Salon & grooming services

On-site appointments for haircuts, styling, and personal care.

Scheduled transportation

Rides to appointments, errands, and community outings.

Front desk services

A friendly, attentive presence for residents, families, and visitors alike.

Community & grounds

04

Landscaped gardens & walking paths

Quiet outdoor spaces designed for slow mornings and easy conversation.

Private courtyard & gathering porch

A shaded spot for residents and visiting family to sit together.

Library & quiet reading rooms

A calm corner stocked with books, puzzles, and good light.

Religious services

On-site services and visits from local clergy for residents who wish to attend.

Pet-friendly community

Because a wagging tail is part of what makes a place feel like home.

Engagement programs

A rotating calendar of activities, music, and outings for every interest.
